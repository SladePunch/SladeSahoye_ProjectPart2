Travel Resources

We know how financially draining travelling can be, so we've compiled a list of resources to ensure the only thing you have to worry about is how much fun you'll have on your travels!

Cheap Travel

  • Skyscanner (Flights)
  • Booking.com (Hotels)
  • RentalCars.com (Cars)
Not all airlines appear in all flight search engines, but they do on Skyscanner. Our favorite feature of Skyscanner is the "everywhere" destination choice, which is ideal for determining where you can fly for the cheapest price! You can also choose an entire country (ex. fly to Italy). Skyscanner also allows you to search within a month if your travel dates are flexible.
We use Booking.com to book hotels. Excellent hotel choices, user-friendly design, consistently reasonable costs, and no fees.
RentalCars.com shows you all the car rentals available across a variety of platforms, making sure you get the cheapest price every time.

Money While Travelling

  • World Nomads (Insurance)
  • Charles Schwab Debit Card (American Traveller)
  • Wise (All Nationalities)
  • Chase Sapphire Preferred Credit Card (Best Visa)
World Nomads Travel Insurance is excellent and can be renewed as many times as you like; submitting claims is quick, and the website is easy to use. As with any insurance, check the tiny print to ensure it's right for you
If you are an American who travels regularly, you should use Charles Schwab's Debit Card. Almost many Americans we know do. They refund all ATM fees at the end of each month (even expensive international ATM fees) and do not charge foreign transaction fees. You will never look for an in-network ATM again
You should always keep a backup debit card in your luggage when traveling, and if you don't have one, we recommend setting up an account with Wise. You can have accounts in multiple currencies, and they are the best option for receiving foreign transactions. They also provide you with a contactless card, which is really useful if you are traveling in Europe

For the majority of users, the greatest travel credit card is the Chase Sapphire Preffered Credit Card, which has large sign-up bonuses (60,000 points) and can be redeemed for points in dozens of various partner programs. An excellent card to start with points before moving on to larger bonuses
